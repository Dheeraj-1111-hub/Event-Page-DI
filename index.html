Join us for an exciting online hackathon that brings creativity and technology right to your screens. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just eager to learn, this event is your gateway to tech glory!

Event Details

Date: 26th September 2024

Time: 11.00 A.M. - 2.00 P.M.

Venue: 1st Floor LAB-2

Registration Fee: 120/- for a team of 2 participants

Rules and Regulations

  • 1) Each team should comprise 2 members only.
  • 2) The hackathon will last for 5 hours.
  • 3) Teams must develop a working prototype based on the theme provided at the start of the event.
  • 4) Projects will be judged based on innovation, feasibility, and execution.
  • 5) Any form of plagiarism or use of pre-built project templates will lead to disqualification.
  • 6) Active participation and collaboration through the online platform are required.
  • 7) Selection criteria for the winners will be based on the best-of-three or knockout format.

Awards and Prizes

There will be a first prize and a second prize.
